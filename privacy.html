Privacy Policy for Analytico

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Analytico (the "Application", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the information obtained from users via the TikTok API.

1. What Data We Collect

Upon receiving your explicit consent through the TikTok authorization mechanism, we request and collect the following types of data based on the permissions you grant:

  • Profile Information (scopes: user.info.basic, user.info.profile): We access your basic identification data such as your Open ID, username, display name, and avatar. We also access your public profile information including your bio description, a web link to your profile, and your verification status (is_verified).
  • Profile Statistics (scope: user.info.stats): We collect your key profile metrics, including your follower count, following count, total like count (hearts), and total video count.
  • Video Data (scope: video.list): We access a list of your public videos, along with statistics for each video, including view count, like count, comment count, and share count.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively to provide you with the core features of our Application: For Analytics and Reporting, and For Personalization and Display.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information... We do not sell or transfer your personal data to any third parties.

4. Deleting Your Data

You have full control over your data. You can revoke our Application's access... To request the complete deletion of all historical data..., please send an email to stasg20000@gmail.com.

5. Contact Information
