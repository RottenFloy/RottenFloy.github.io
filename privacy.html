Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how our service (the "Application", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects the information obtained from users via the TikTok API.

1. What Data We Collect

Upon receiving your explicit consent through the TikTok authorization mechanism, we request and collect the following types of data based on the permissions you grant:

  • Profile Information (scopes: user.info.basic and user.info.profile): We access your basic identification data such as your Open ID, username, display name, and avatar. We also access your public profile information including your bio description, a web link to your profile, and your verification status (is_verified).
  • Profile Statistics (scope: user.info.stats): We collect your key profile metrics, including your follower count, following count, total like count (hearts), and total video count.
  • Video Data (scope: video.list): We access a list of your public videos, along with statistics for each video, including view count, like count, comment count, and share count.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively to provide you with the core features of our Application:

  • For Analytics and Reporting: The statistical data is used to generate analytical reports on your account and video performance, build dynamic charts, and calculate engagement rates.
  • For Personalization and Display: Your profile information (username, avatar, bio, etc.) is used for display within our Telegram bot's interface so you can easily identify and manage the connected profile.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Data is stored on secure servers and is used only to perform the functions described above. We do not sell or transfer your personal data to any third parties.

4. Deleting Your Data

You have full control over your data. You can revoke our Application's access to your account at any time by following these steps:

  1. Go to your profile settings in the TikTok app.
  2. Find the "Security" or "Manage app permissions" section.
  3. Find our application in the list and revoke its access.

To request the complete deletion of all historical data we have stored on our servers, please send an email to stasg20000@gmail.com. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: stasg20000@gmail.com.
